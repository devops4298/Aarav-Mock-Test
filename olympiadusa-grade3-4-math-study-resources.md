## 4. Suggested Order of Use

1. **Diagnostic (Week 1):** Pick 8–10 easy (1–2 point) questions from the Dec 2024 OlympiadUSA PDF. Untimed, framed as puzzles. Note what he already knows.
2. **Teach one topic at a time (Weeks 2+):** Khan Academy lesson → a few practice exercises → 2–3 olympiad questions on that same topic. Suggested teaching order: Topics 1–2 (arithmetic) → 3 (word problems) → 6 (measurement) → 7 (perimeter/area) → 4 (patterns) → 5 (fractions) → 10 (early algebra) → 8–9 (spatial & logic, sprinkle throughout as fun breaks).
3. **Mixed practice:** Edugain worksheets + Math Kangaroo samples.
4. **Full papers (final phase):** OlympiadUSA past papers — untimed first, then with the 90-minute clock.

**Pacing:** 20–30 minutes a day, 3–4 days a week beats long weekend sessions at this age.
